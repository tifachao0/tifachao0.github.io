What is a Digital Painting?

A digital painting is a work of art that is created in similar fashion to its traditional counterpart, but with computer software as the primary media instead of paint. Within an image editing application such as Photoshop, a user may select from a variety of brushes, and then apply color to a virtual canvas using a graphics tablet or drawing tablet as the preferred input device.

Where to Find Digital Paintings

Digital paintings can be found in video games, television, films, advertisements and other forms of digital media, from rough concept art for films such as Avatar to finished illustrations for playing card games such as Magic the Gathering. In summary, concept artists and illustrators play a large part in the entertainment industry.
