Step 1: Create

Iris Origami is a website where you can learn how to fold your favorite origami animals, flowers, vehicles and more! Decorate your room, create a bouquet, give a gift to a friend or family member - there are many ways to incorporate origami into your everyday life.

If you are new to folding origami or looking for a refresher, you can learn about stores that supply origami paper and origami diagram notation here.

Step 2: Share

Other users from around the world, from first-timers to hobbyists, are also learning how to fold origami with you! Share your creations online by uploading photographs for the origami model of your choice. You can also view the origami models that other users have created.
